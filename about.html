About

RobTechUK was founded in March 2026 in Manchester, England by Robbie Adamson, a novice keen to learn tech. One man, one mission.

Whilst working a minimum wage factory job, I had some ideas for games that I thought would be popular. I set about contacting some tech specialists about creating some of my games, but was quoted £10,000–40,000 for some, and one required a gambling licence.

I had no tech experience but started to think, ‘How hard is it to create games?’ After several AI chatbot conversations, I decided to go at it alone.

In true punk rock style, I got myself a laptop and some second-hand books and decided to do it myself. Starting with the simplest of word games to learn game logic, Letters In was started as a simple word game to learn games logic and design. RobTechUK is the start of something big…
