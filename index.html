How to Play

Time: You have 200 seconds to score as many points as possible.

Move: Tap a tile next to the empty space to move it.

Play: Tap Freeze Grid, then swipe to select words.

  • Words must be 3+ letters
  • English words only (no names)
  • Across and down only
  • Each word used once
  • Same word allowed across & down
  • 3 letters = 1 point
  • 4 letters = 2 points
  • 5 letters = 3 points
  • +1 bonus per extra word per freeze
  • +5 chain bonus (AIR → HAIR → CHAIR)
