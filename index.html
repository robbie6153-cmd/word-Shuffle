How to Play

Time: You have 200 seconds to score as many points as possible.

Move: Tap a tile next to the empty space to move it.

Play: Tap Freeze Grid, then swipe to select words.

Rules

  • Words must be 3 letters or more
  • Words must be in the English dictionary
  • No names, places, brands, abbreviations, or words that would normally begin with a capital letter
  • Only words across and down can be selected
  • Each word can only be used once
  • The same word may be used once across and once down

Scoring

  • 3 letters = 1 point
  • 4 letters = 2 points
  • 5 letters = 3 points
  • +1 combo bonus for each extra word found during the same frozen grid
  • Combo bonus increases by 1 for each additional word found while the grid is frozen
  • +5 bonus for a chain word (AIR → HAIR → CHAIR)
  • There will always be at least one chain word
  • Each chain can only be scored once, either across or down

About Word Shuffle

Word Shuffle is designed to test both speed and logical thinking. Spotting words quickly is important, but the highest scores come from arranging the grid carefully to create combo bonuses and chain words. The real challenge is not just seeing words — it is planning the grid in the smartest way before you freeze it.
