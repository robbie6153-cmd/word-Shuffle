How to Play

Time: You have 200 seconds to score as many points as possible.

Move: Tap a tile next to the empty space to move it.

Play: Tap Freeze Grid, then swipe to select words.

  • Words must be 3+ letters
  • English words only (no names)
  • Only words across and downwards can be swiped
  • Each word can only be used once
  • However, the same word can be used across & down
  • 3 letters = 1 point
  • 4 letters = 2 points
  • 5 letters = 3 points
  • +1 combo bonus for each extra word per frozen screen
  • Combo bonus increases by one point for each word found in the grid whilst frozen
  • +5 bonus for a chain word (AIR → HAIR → CHAIR)
  • There will always be at least one chain word
  • You can only have the same chain word once, across or down
